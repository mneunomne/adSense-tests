AdSense Tests

From the time I first contact you to the time my dispute is finalized, what is the time frame of this whole process?

We typically say it takes about a month, however every case is unique. Sometimes it could take two weeks, other times it could take several months.

Will the hospital place my bill into collections?

When you are actively negotiating with the hospital and are trying to resolve the billing dispute, it is unlikely they will place your bill into collections.

How much money to you typically save?

Every case is unique. The size of the bills is always different so therefore the size of the savings is always different. The larger the bill you have, the more we can save you.

How much of my medical background will you have?

We will only have what you give us. If there are parts of your medical history you are uncomfortable sharing, that’s fine with us. We only want what is relevant to our case.

Will I get the documents that I sent you back?

No, our policy is to keep everything you send us. We ask that you photocopy everything you send us.

What does Dispute need from me?

First, any medicals you have received and you want to dispute.

What if I already paid my medical bill and I think they made a mistake, am I still eligible to dispute my bill?

Sometimes. This is a time sensitive issue. So if it has been years since you paid your bill, chances are we will be out of luck. If it has been less than a year, we will have a better chance.

My loved one has passed away, can we still dispute their medical bills?

Yes, if you feel you are being overcharged during your time of grief, then we will absolutely dispute on your behalf.

How much will Dispute charge me?

There are two different ways you can choose to pay us. You can opt into our hourly plan which costs $100 per hr. or you can choose our contingency plan which charges 30% of your total savings.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes, when you refer someone and we end up saving them money, you and the person you referred get awarded. We will send checks to both of you.
We also have a referral program setup for companies. If you would like to work with us please visit our partnership page to understand more about our point system or give us a call.

What if all my bills are correct, but I still want to lower my bill. Can dispute help me?

Yes, it is very common for hospitals to negotiate on bills. Our expect negotiators can call on your behalf and try and lower your bill.

Is there an age limit on the bills? If so, what is the oldest bill you will work with?

There is no age limit. We will work with any bill. It doesn’t matter to us how long you have had it.
